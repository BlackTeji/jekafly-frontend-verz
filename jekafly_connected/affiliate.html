Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How long does approval take?
We review applications within 48 hours on business days. Once approved you will receive an email with your referral link and dashboard access.
When and how do I get paid?
Earnings are paid weekly to the Nigerian bank account you provide. There is no minimum threshold — every naira earned goes to you.
What counts as a conversion?
A conversion is when a referred client completes and pays for a visa application. You earn commission on the application fee paid.
Can I apply without a large following?
Yes! Travel agents, school counsellors, relocation consultants, and anyone with an engaged network can earn with us regardless of follower count.
How does the referral link work?
You get a unique link like jekafly.com/?ref=YOURCODE. When someone clicks it, a 30-day cookie is set. If they complete an application within 30 days, you earn the commission.
